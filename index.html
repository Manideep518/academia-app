Academia App

Academia is the best eLearning website template that helps create a powerful online education platform. The tool comes with many practical features that you can take to your total advantage. For starters, Academia includes a beautiful design and many different front and internal page layouts to work with. Speaking of layouts, it is a Bootstrap 5 template, so you know your website will work great across all modern devices.

Moreover, Academia features CTA buttons, transparent header, hover effects, animations, carousels and student testimonials/reviews. Additionally, you also get pricing tables, social icons, Google Maps integration and a working contact form, amongst many more in between. Establish your own eLearning website with Academia and make an immediate difference once and for all.
